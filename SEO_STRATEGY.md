# PristinePhysio SEO Strategy & Implementation Plan

This document outlines the comprehensive SEO strategy for PristinePhysio to dominate local search rankings and attract high-intent patients.

## 1. Keyword Research & Targeting

### Core Keywords (High Volume)
- "Physiotherapy near me"
- "Physical therapy [City Name]"
- "Physio clinic [City Name]"
- "Sports injury clinic"

### Service-Specific Keywords (High Intent)
- "ACL recovery exercises"
- "Post-surgery rehabilitation"
- "Chronic back pain treatment"
- "Manual therapy for neck pain"
- "Pediatric physiotherapy services"

### Long-Tail Keywords (Educational/Blog)
- "How to recover from a hamstring strain"
- "Benefits of dry needling for athletes"
- "Exercises for seniors to improve balance"
- "When to see a physio after surgery"

---

## 2. On-Page Optimization Recommendations

### Homepage
- **Title Tag**: PristinePhysio | Expert Physiotherapy & Sports Injury Clinic in [City]
- **Meta Description**: Restore your movement at PristinePhysio. Expert-led physiotherapy, sports rehab, and personalized recovery plans in [City]. Book your consultation today.
- **H1 Header**: Must contain "Physiotherapy" and "[City]".
- **Content**: Include a clear value proposition, trust signals (reviews, certifications), and a direct CTA.

### Services Pages (Individual)
- Each service (e.g., Sports Rehab) should have its own dedicated URL (e.g., `/services/sports-rehabilitation`).
- **H1**: Sports Injury Rehabilitation in [City]
- **Content**: 500+ words explaining the service, techniques used, and what to expect.

### About Us
- Focus on the "E-E-A-T" (Experience, Expertise, Authoritativeness, Trustworthiness).
- Include bios of therapists with their credentials and specializations.

---

## 3. Local SEO Strategy

### Google Business Profile (GBP)
- **Claim & Verify**: The most critical step for local ranking.
- **Optimization**: Use high-quality photos of the clinic, list all services, and keep hours updated.
- **Posts**: Weekly updates on health tips or clinic news.

### Local Citations (NAP Consistency)
- Ensure Name, Address, and Phone (NAP) are identical across:
  - Yelp
  - Healthgrades
  - Yellow Pages
  - Local Chamber of Commerce
  - Industry-specific directories (e.g., APTA directory)

### Review Management Plan
- **In-Clinic Signage**: QR codes at the reception desk linking to the Google Review page.
- **Follow-up Emails**: Automated emails 24 hours after a session asking for feedback.
- **Response Strategy**: Respond to *every* review (both positive and negative) professionally.
